Getting Started

What is BetterType AI Keyboard?
BetterType AI Keyboard is an AI-powered keyboard for iOS that helps you write better across all your apps. It offers grammar correction, tone adjustment in 22+ styles, AI-powered writing assistance, and translation to 44+ languages - all directly from your keyboard.
How do I install the BetterType keyboard?
After downloading the app:
  1. Open the BetterType app and complete the onboarding
  2. Go to iOS Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Keyboard ‚Üí Keyboards
  3. Tap "Add New Keyboard"
  4. Select "BetterType" from the list
  5. Enable "Allow Full Access" for best functionality
  6. Tap the globe icon üåê in any app to switch to BetterType
Why does the keyboard need "Full Access"?
Full Access allows BetterType to send your text to AI models for processing (grammar fixes, tone changes, translations, etc.). Without it, the keyboard cannot provide AI-powered features. Your privacy is protected - see our Privacy section below for details.
Which apps work with BetterType?
BetterType works with virtually any iOS app that accepts text input, including Instagram, WhatsApp, Telegram, Gmail, Slack, Discord, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, iMessage, Reddit, TikTok, Snapchat, Tinder, Bumble, Hinge, Notes, and many more.

Features

What's the difference between the 4 main features?
Grammar Fix: Corrects spelling, grammar, and punctuation errors while preserving your original meaning and tone

Change Tone: Rewrites your text in 22 different styles (Formal, Casual, Friendly, Confident, Professional, Enthusiastic, etc.)

Ask AI: Get intelligent answers to questions or help writing content from scratch

Translation: Translate text between 44+ languages instantly
What tones are available?
BetterType offers 22 tones across 4 categories:

  • Emotional: Enthusiastic, Empathetic, Motivational, Calming, Fun, Inspirational
  • Creative: Storytelling, Educational, Promotional, Testimonial, Creative

Can I control the length of responses?
Yes! Most features offer content size options:

  • Structured: Organized with formatting (for certain tones)

Does BetterType support multiple response alternatives?
Yes! For many features, BetterType generates multiple variants of responses, allowing you to choose the option that best fits your needs.

Subscription & Pricing

What subscription tiers does BetterType offer?
BetterType offers three tiers:
  • Free: Limited access to basic features
  • Starter: Full access to Grammar Fix and Tone Change features
  • Pro: All features including Ask AI (premium feature), unlimited usage, and all future features
Is there a free trial?
Yes! New users get a 3-day free trial to experience all Pro features before committing to a subscription.
What are the payment options?
BetterType offers both monthly and yearly subscription billing. Yearly subscriptions typically offer better value with significant savings compared to monthly billing.
How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel:
  1. Open iOS Settings
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
  3. Go to Subscriptions
  4. Select BetterType
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Privacy & Security

Is my data private and secure?
Yes. BetterType offers two privacy modes:
Privacy Mode: Maximum privacy with no usage data collection

Help Improve Mode: Anonymous usage data to improve the product (no sensitive content stored)
Your typed text is sent to AI providers (like Google Gemini) for processing but is never stored by BetterType. API keys and sensitive data are stored securely in iOS Keychain.
What data does BetterType collect?
BetterType collects:
  • Never Collected: Your actual keyboard input/text content
  • Optionally Collected (if you enable "Help Improve"): Anonymous feature usage analytics, app integration data, subscription status
  • Always Secure: Payment information (handled by Apple), subscription details
Which AI models does BetterType use?
BetterType uses state-of-the-art AI models from multiple providers:
  • Google Gemini (primary provider)
  • Support for OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, and Grok models
  • Different models are optimized for different tasks (grammar uses lightweight models, creative tasks use more capable models)
Does BetterType work offline?
No, BetterType requires an internet connection to access AI models for text processing. However, subscription validation has offline fallback to ensure the keyboard works even with temporary connection issues.

Keyboard Usage & Troubleshooting

How do I switch between BetterType and my default keyboard?
Tap the globe icon üåê on your keyboard to cycle through all installed keyboards, including BetterType and your system keyboard.
The keyboard isn't showing up. What should I do?
Check these common issues:
  • Ensure you've added BetterType in Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Keyboard ‚Üí Keyboards
  • Verify "Allow Full Access" is enabled
  • Restart the app you're typing in
  • Restart your iPhone if the issue persists
Why can't I use Ask AI?
Ask AI is a premium feature available only to Pro subscribers. Upgrade your subscription in the app to unlock this feature.
The keyboard feels slow. How can I fix this?
AI processing takes a few seconds. If it's unusually slow:
  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure you're on the latest version of BetterType
  • Try restarting the app
  • Contact support if the issue persists
Can I use BetterType as my default keyboard?
Yes! iOS allows you to set BetterType as your primary keyboard, though you can always switch back using the globe icon. However, for security-sensitive fields (like passwords), iOS automatically switches to the system keyboard.
