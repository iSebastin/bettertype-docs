Common Issues & Solutions

Keyboard Not Showing Up

If BetterType keyboard isn't appearing:

  1. Open iOS Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Keyboard ‚Üí Keyboards
  2. Tap "Add New Keyboard" and select "BetterType"
  3. Enable "Allow Full Access" for AI features
  4. Tap the globe icon üåê in any app to switch to BetterType

AI Features Not Working

If grammar fixes or tone changes aren't working:

  1. Ensure "Allow Full Access" is enabled in iOS Settings
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Restart the app you're typing in
  4. Update BetterType to the latest version

Subscription Issues

For subscription or payment problems:

  1. Verify your subscription status in iOS Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions
  2. Ensure your payment method is valid
  3. Try restoring purchases in the BetterType app
  4. Contact us at theredarrowapp@gmail.com with your issue

App Running Slow

If the keyboard feels sluggish:

  1. Check your internet connection speed
  2. Close other apps running in the background
  3. Restart your iPhone
  4. Ensure you're running iOS 18.2 or later

Can't Access Premium Features

If you're subscribed but can't access Pro features:

  1. Open BetterType app and tap "Restore Purchases"
  2. Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase
  3. Check your subscription is active in iOS Settings
  4. Try logging out and back into your Apple ID
