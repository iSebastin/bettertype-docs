Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 03, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how BetterType: AI Keyboard ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS keyboard extension application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Text input that you type while using the keyboard extension
  • Platform and tone preferences you select
  • Favorites and customizations you save
  • Settings and configuration choices

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or tracking information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Contact lists or address book information
  • Photos, videos, or other media files
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Browsing history or search queries

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

2.1 Text Processing

  • We send only the text you type to OpenAI's GPT-4o API for AI-powered text generation
  • Your text is processed to generate improved versions based on your selected platform and tone
  • No text is stored permanently on our servers
  • Text is not used for any purpose other than generating your requested content

2.2 Local Storage

  • Your preferences (favorites, settings, customizations) are stored locally on your device
  • This information is not transmitted to our servers
  • Local storage helps provide a personalized experience without requiring accounts

2.3 API Key Management

  • Your OpenAI API key is stored securely on your device
  • We do not have access to your API key
  • API communications are encrypted using HTTPS

3. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

3.1 Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API: We send your text input to OpenAI for AI processing
  • No other third-party services receive your data
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your information

3.2 Legal Requirements

  • We may disclose information if required by law or legal process
  • We may disclose information to protect our rights or safety
  • We will notify you of any such disclosure when possible

4. DATA SECURITY

4.1 Security Measures

  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • Local data is protected by iOS security features
  • API keys are stored securely using iOS Keychain

4.2 Data Retention

  • Text input is not stored after processing
  • Local preferences are retained until you delete the app
  • No server-side data retention

5. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

5.1 Access and Control
